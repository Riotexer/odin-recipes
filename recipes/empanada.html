Empanadas

Description

This is a very traditional recipe in Argentina. There're a lot of variants, but here we are going to be covering the one filled with meat

Ingredients

  • Empanadas dough (disks)
  • Cut up Meat
  • 1 Onion
  • 1/2 pepper
  • White Wine
  • Spices
  • 1 Egg
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Seal the cut up meat in an oiled pan
  2. Add the Sliced Onion and Pepper
  3. Add a little of White Wine and the Spices, then let it cook for 8 min
  4. Grab a dough and "paint" the borders with the mixed Egg
  5. Add some of the Meat mix to the dough
  6. Fold the dough by the middle sealing the borders
  7. Take all of the filled doughs to the oven, let it cook for 20 minutes or until the doughs turned gold
